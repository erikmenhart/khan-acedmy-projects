Plain Pasta

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
pasta a lot
salt pinch
water fill according to water
olive oil 1 second pour

Step 1: Boil water with salt and oil.
Step 2: add pasta once boiling, wait 9-10 mins or until tender.
Step 3: add more oil then serve.

Source: plain pasta
